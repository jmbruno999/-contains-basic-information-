Our Hair and Makeup Services

Our team comes to you, at the venue of your choice, so that you and your bridal party can relax and be pampered as we create the look of your dreams. We are based on the Central Coast, in San Luis Obispo, and we travel between San Francisco and Los Angeles. We also do out-of-state weddings by request

Specializing in Airbrush Makeup, Eyelash Extensions, and Hair Extensions

Our team is a team of artists and experts. We use only the highest quality products that we have tested to withstand all the elements of special events. We specialize in airbrush makeup, so your skin looks flawless and is photo-ready. We also professionally apply eyelash extensions and hair extensions, using the latest techniques. We invite you to see examples of our work.

Our Process Begins with a Consultation and Hair and Makeup Trial

We consider it an honor to work with you. Our process begins with a consultation, where we discuss your desired look. We take our time reviewing sample photos with you, until we’ve settled upon the look that you desire. We then set up a trial day of hair and makeup, so we can ensure you are pleased with our creation. Our mission, above all, is that you are absolutely pleased with your hair and makeup. We are here to serve you. See what our happy brides have to say about our services.
